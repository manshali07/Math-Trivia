Addition

Addition (often signified by the plus symbol "+") of two whole numbers is the total amount of those values combined.

Addition has several important properties:
1. Commutative, meaning that order does not matter.

2. Associative, meaning that when one adds more than two numbers, the order in which addition is performed does not matter.

3. Identity Element,addition of 0 does not change a number.
